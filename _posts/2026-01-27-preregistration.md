We are excited to announce that registration is now open for the workshop, “Preregistration in Practice”. In this session, Olmo van den Akker will focus on 
evaluation and practical design of preregistrations for empirical research. He will focus on the core principles of preregistration and common misconceptions and limitations of preregistration.

A considerable part of this workshop is dedicated to hands-on activities to read, evaluate, and critique preregistrations, as well as drafting or improving preregistration for participants upcoming studies.
For further details about this workshop, click [here](https://paulmeehlschool.github.io/workshops/preregistration/). Please note that the workshop will be **exclusively in-person** on **February 19, 2026**. We look forward to seeing you in Eindhoven!

You can register for this workshop by completing the [Registration Form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=R_J9zM5gD0qddXBM9g78ZP_Kihp-VglPgWom9gajHXdUODY0VlJORjJFWUExVlczSzhFUU1PVDVLOC4u). Please make sure to register before <ins>**February 13**</ins>.
