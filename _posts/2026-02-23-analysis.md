We are excited to announce that registration is now open for the workshop, “Advanced Basic Statistics Workshop: Deepening Your Understanding of Standard Statistical Analyses”. In this session, Chris Snijders and Antal Haans will focus on 
changing the autopilot use of regression models and ANOVA. They will cover topics like the link between research question and the chosen statistical procedure, contrast in ANOVA, common analytical pitfalls, and conventions behind factorial ANOVA.

This workshop combines conceptual discussion with hands-on exercises and real-world examples, but does not cover basics of regression models and ANOVA and aims at people who already know and use these methods. 
For further details about this workshop, click [here](https://paulmeehlschool.github.io/workshops/analysis-knowledge/). Please note that the workshop will be **exclusively in-person** on **March 27, 2026**. We look forward to seeing you in Eindhoven!
