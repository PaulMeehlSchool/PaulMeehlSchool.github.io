We are excited to announce that registration is now open for the workshop, “Computational Cognitive Models”. In this session, Leendert van Maanen and Dominik Bachmann will focus on a
conceptual introduction to computational cognitive models with emphasis on evidence accumulation models. Other than offering hands-on experience with fitting and drawing inference from these models,
topics like what cognitive models are, when and why they are useful and how they are used in practice will be discussed in this workshop.

For further details about this workshop, click [here](https://paulmeehlschool.github.io/workshops/cognitive-model/). Please note that the workshop will be **exclusively in-person** on **April 20, 2026**. We look forward to seeing you in Eindhoven!

You can register for this workshop by completing the [Registration Form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=R_J9zM5gD0qddXBM9g78ZP_Kihp-VglPgWom9gajHXdURDJCVEtNQTVXVzkxMVFESEUzUEpHWEE4Ni4u).
