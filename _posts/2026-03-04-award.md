---

We are pleased to announce that the Paul Meehl Graduate School is launching the **PMGS Dissertation Award**, recognizing outstanding PhD dissertations that advance meta-research and demonstrate a strong commitment to rigorous and responsible research practices.

The winner will receive a €1,000 prize and will be invited to give a keynote presentation at the PMGS Meta-Research Symposium.

To learn more about eligibility and the application procedure, see: [https://paulmeehlschool.github.io/award/](https://paulmeehlschool.github.io/award/).
