We are excited to announce that registration is now open for the workshop, "Theoretical Agent-Based Models". In this session, Matteo Michelini will focus on Agent-Based Models as
a tool for theory-building and exploratory testing. The morning session will focus on conceptual underestanding of these models, as well as exploring classic models (such as the 
Zollman effect and epistemic landscapes) and their underlying assumptions. The afternoon session will focus on how to make these models more open to allow other to  evaluate, criticize, and extend them.

For further details about this workshop, click [here](https://paulmeehlschool.github.io/workshops/agent-model/). Please note that the workshop will be **exclusively in-person** on **May 27, 2026**. 
We look forward to seeing you in Eindhoven!
